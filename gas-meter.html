GAS METER

Our gas meters are designed to meet the highest standards, compliant with the STS Standard. With automatic valve control and prepaid functionality, our meters provide a seamless and efficient approach to managing gas consumption. ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certifications guarantee quality and environmental responsibility.

Benefits

Efficient Consumption Management

Our gas meters empower users to monitor and manage their gas consumption efficiently, reducing waste and promoting responsible usage.

Easy Top-Up

The integration with Olivia vending software allows for a straightforward and quick top-up process using 20-digit tokens, ensuring uninterrupted gas supply.

Secure and Reliable

Automatic valve control enhances safety by allowing for the automatic shut-off of gas flow when needed, providing peace of mind for users.

Key features

  • STS Standard Compliance: Our gas meters adhere to the Standard Transfer Specification, ensuring compatibility and reliability.
  • {/* Certifications: ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certified for quality and environmental management. */} Automatic Valve Control: Experience hassle-free gas management with automatic valve control.
  • Prepaid Functionality: Manage your gas usage efficiently through prepaid options.
  • Top-Up with Olivia Vending Software: Conveniently reload your gas meter with 20-digit tokens using Olivia vending software.
  • Communication: Stay connected with our Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI) system through the LORA-RF module.

Standard Gas Meter

  • Nominal Flow Rate 1.6 m³/h: Our gas meters feature a nominal flow rate of 1.6 cubic meters per hour, ensuring accurate measurement and billing for gas usage.
  • Optional External Keypad: For enhanced user interaction and accessibility, we offer an optional external keypad. This keypad provides an alternative interface for managing the gas meter, making it even more user-friendly.

Configurations

Without CIU/UIU: This configuration simplifies the setup, making it suitable for users who prefer basic functionality. With

CIU/UIU: Opt for the version with a Customer Interface Unit (CIU) or User Interface Unit (UIU) for enhanced features and user interaction. This option provides a more comprehensive experience, including detailed usage data and analytics.

Top up

Top up effortlessly using Olivia's vending software and a 20-digit Token. Stay connected to your usage, and rest easy with ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 certifications, reflecting our commitment to the environment.
