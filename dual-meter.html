DUAL TARIFF ELECTRICITY METER

Introducing the Hacom Energy Dual Source Meter – Empowering You with Control and Flexibility!
Our Dual Tariff Meter is designed to put the power back in your hands. It allows you to independently manage each source of energy with ease and precision.

ONE SINGLE METER, DUAL POWER CAPABILITIES!

Picture this: A single meter that effortlessly manages both NEPA electricity and generator power, offering you the ultimate in versatility and control. With the power to set different tariffs for each energy source and the added convenience of automatic source switching, it's a game-changer in the world of energy management. Welcome to the future of power!

Experience the Pinnacle of Energy Management

Our Wall Mounted Single Phase STS Meter is the epitome of electricity metering technology, offering seamless top-up options, cutting-edge connectivity through the LoRa-RF module, and the flexibility to operate in both postpaid and prepaid modes. It ensures precise and reliable single-source metering for your single-phase electricity consumption, all designed to empower your energy management.

Key features

  • Automatic Source Detection: Our Dual Source Meter intelligently detects the most cost-effective energy source, eliminating the need for manual switching and ensuring uninterrupted power.
  • Transparency and Fairness: Introduce a base fee for transparent and equitable billing, so you only pay for the energy you consume.
  • Tailored Tariffs: You have the flexibility to set distinct tariffs for electricity from the grid and your generator, allowing for accurate cost tracking and fair billing.
  • Efficient Energy Management: The Dual Source Meter enables you to build and manage each power source independently, reducing waste and enhancing efficiency.
  • Cost Savings: By using the most cost-effective energy source, you can significantly reduce your energy expenses.
  • Reliability: Say goodbye to inconvenient blackouts. Our meter guarantees a consistent and reliable power supply.

Low Voltage (LV) Maximum Demand Meters:

Versatility is key, and our LV maximum demand meters provide it. They support both prepaid and postpaid billing modes, making them suitable for various applications.

High Voltage (HV) Maximum Demand Meters:

These three-phase meters are engineered for high voltage applications, offering the same advanced features as our LV meters while adapting to the unique demands of high voltage environments.
