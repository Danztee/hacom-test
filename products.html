MINI GRID

Mini grids predominantly depend on solar panels to harness sunlight and transform it into electrical power. This sustainable energy resource is not only plentiful but also eco-friendly. By implementing mini grids with solar technology, communities gain control over their energy supply, reducing their dependence on non-renewable energy sources and contributing to a cleaner and more sustainable future.
