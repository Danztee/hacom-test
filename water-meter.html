WATER METER

Our prepaid water meter is a cutting-edge solution for efficient water management, allowing users to take control of their water consumption, budget, and environmental impact. It offers a range of advanced features and benefits for both residential and commercial applications.

BENEFITS

Flexible Billing Options

Our prepaid water meter offers a range of billing options to cater to your individual preferences and requirements. You can choose between prepaid and post-paid billing, ensuring that our water meter aligns seamlessly with your billing needs. Whether you prefer paying in advance or after consumption, our system adapts to your choice, offering you complete flexibility.

Non-Return Valve for Back-Flow Prevention

Inside the water meter, you'll find a non-return valve—a crucial component designed to prevent back-flow. This valve ensures that water flows in only one direction, preventing contamination and preserving water quality. It's a valuable feature that enhances the integrity of your water supply system.

Advanced Security Measures:

We prioritize the security of your water supply. Our water meter is equipped with an anti-tamper mechanism, which safeguards the meter from unauthorized access or tampering attempts. In the event of tampering, the meter will automatically close the valve to maintain the security of your water infrastructure.

Magnetic Field Detection and Cover Event:

In addition to anti-tamper features, our water meter is equipped with advanced security measures. It includes a magnetic field detection system that identifies any unusual magnetic interference. Moreover, it can detect open cover events, ensuring that any unauthorized access is promptly addressed.

Key features

  • STS Standard Compliance: Our prepaid water meter adheres to the highest industry standards, ensuring precise measurement and seamless compatibility with various water systems.
  • Motorized Valves for Precise Control: The inclusion of motorized valves allows you to have fine-grained control over your water usage. Turn the flow on or off as needed to make the most of your water resources.
  • High Accuracy Measurement: Our STS standard water meter provides incredibly accurate measurements, ensuring you have a clear understanding of your water consumption.
  • Token-Based Recharge: Topping up your water credit is a breeze with our 20-digit TOKEN system. Simply input the token, and your meter is ready to go.
  • Low Credit Alarm: The built-in low credit alarm feature helps users stay informed about their water credit. It can be customized by inputting a corresponding TOKEN, ensuring no unexpected interruptions in water supply.
  • Environmental Stewardship: By offering precise control over water consumption, our meter promotes responsible water use and reduces wastage, contributing to environmental sustainability.
  • Budget Control: Users can effectively manage their water expenses, preventing unexpected bill shocks and promoting financial responsibility.
  • Remote Valve Control: The ability to remotely control the water valve is a valuable feature, especially for properties requiring remote access and management, such as vacation homes.
  • Anti-Tamper Protection: The meter is equipped with anti-tamper features, and it will automatically close the valve upon detecting any magnetic field or unauthorized cover access, ensuring the integrity of your water supply.
  • User-Friendly LCD Display: The Liquid Crystal Display (LCD) screen provides users with a clear and easy-to-read interface to access essential information about their water usage.
  • Seamless Connectivity: Connect your meter with the Olivia AMI system using LORA RF or Bluetooth 4.0 on request. This allows you to monitor data such as Credit Remaining, Total Purchased, and Total Credit by inputting a short code.
