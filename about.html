Committed to Redefining Utility Management with Cutting-Edge Solutions.

At Hacom Energy, we go beyond smart metering solutions. Our core focus is on developing state-of-the-art, AI-empowered technology for efficient and sustainable resource management. Specializing in gas, electricity, and water metering, we provide the tools for optimizing your utility resources.

Our Mission

Our mission is to evolve from providing reliable metering solutions to becoming a dynamic utility company. We are committed to offering comprehensive utility services that empower our customers to manage their resource consumption efficiently. Our dedication lies in preventing revenue losses, promoting sustainability, and enhancing the overall customer experience in the world of utilities.

Our Vision

At Hacom Energy, our vision is to lead the global utility industry by providing individuals and businesses with advanced solutions for managing gas, electricity, and water resources. We foresee a future where innovative utility technology is central to achieving sustainability and responsible resource consumption. By embracing innovation, environmental responsibility, and user-centric design, we aim to revolutionize the way the world accesses and manages essential resources through our utility services.

What We Offer

At Hacom, we offer a comprehensive range of smart metering solutions designed to enhance resource management and streamline the billing process. Our offerings include:

Gas Metering Solutions:

  • Our gas meters are precision-engineered for accurate measurement and control of gas consumption.
  • Prepaid and postpaid options cater to a wide range of customer needs.
  • Smart technology allows for remote monitoring and control.

Electricity Metering Solutions:

  • Our electricity meters are built to provide precise tracking of energy usage
  • Both residential and industrial meters ensure cost-effective energy management.
  • Remote communication capability for effortless monitoring and billing.

Water Metering Solutions:

  • Our water meters contribute to responsible water usage and reduced water bills.
  • Non-return valves prevent backflow, enhancing conservation efforts.
  • Anti-tamper features protect against unauthorized access.
