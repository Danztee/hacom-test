Hassle-Free Gas Solution

we're transforming the way you think about cooking gas. Our Gas as a Service (GaaS) solutions provide a continuous and convenient gas supply, eliminating the need for individual cylinder purchases. With a commitment to cost savings, convenience, and environmental responsibility, we're here to revolutionize the way you use and manage gas.

How It Works

We take care of everything! Instead of purchasing and refilling gas tanks, we install a gas storage tank and a smart meter in your home. This tank connects to your appliances, ensuring a seamless flow of gas to every corner of your house. You'll never have to worry about gas again!

Imagine having the freedom to cook, heat water, and run all your gas appliances without ever running low on fuel. Gas as a Service is the ultimate solution for modern, convenient living.

The Advantages

  1. Continuous Gas Supply: Say goodbye to the frustration of running out of gas mid-cooking. With GaaS, there's always a reliable supply of gas available.
  2. Convenient Metering: We take care of the technicalities. Our metering systems accurately track gas usage, ensuring fair billing and eliminating the need for individual cylinder purchases.
  3. Cost Savings: Save money by eliminating the need to buy large gas cylinders. GaaS offers a cost-effective solution that benefits both your wallet and the environment.
  4. Safety Assurance: Hacom Energy prioritizes safety and adheres to all necessary safety standards and regulations.
  5. Dedicated Customer Support: Our customer support team is ready to assist with any inquiries, ensuring your GaaS experience is always exceptional.

No More Delivery Delays

Never be inconvenienced by delivery delays or sudden gas shortages again. Gas as a Service ensures a consistent gas supply to your home without the frustrations of scheduling and waiting for gas cylinder deliveries.

Eco-Friendly

Our system promotes environmental sustainability by reducing the need for gas cylinder transportation and minimizing the risk of gas leaks, making your home safer and greener.

Upgrade Your Lifestyle

Imagine the convenience of seamlessly running gas appliances without interruption. Your cooking, heating, and hot water will all operate effortlessly, enhancing your day-to-day life.
